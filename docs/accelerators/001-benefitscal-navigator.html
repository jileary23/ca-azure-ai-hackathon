ACCEL-001 CDSS

BenefitsCal Navigator Agent

California Department of Social Services — CalFresh, CalWORKs, General Relief, CAPI

Port 8001 / 3001

Problem Statement

The BenefitsCal portal serves millions of Californians seeking public assistance. County welfare offices face persistent backlogs from constantly shifting federal eligibility rules, and over 40% of applicants speak a language other than English. The complexity of navigating CalFresh, CalWORKs, General Relief, and CAPI simultaneously overwhelms both applicants and caseworkers.

Solution Overview

Natural Language Q&A

Answers benefits eligibility questions in plain language with citation-backed responses from CDSS policy manuals.

FPL Pre-Screening

Real-time Federal Poverty Level–based eligibility pre-screening across all four programs.

Multi-Language Support

Full support for 9 languages to serve California's diverse population.

Citation-Backed Answers

Every response includes references to official CDSS policy documents for transparency.

Human Escalation

Confidence-based escalation routes complex cases to human caseworkers.

Program Coverage

CalFresh, CalWORKs, General Relief, and CAPI — all in a single unified experience.

3-Agent Pipeline Architecture

STAGE 1

QueryAgent

  • ▸ Intent detection (eligibility, office lookup, program info)
  • ▸ Entity extraction (county, program, household size)
  • ▸ PII filtering — SSN, income redaction
  • ▸ Language detection
STAGE 2

RouterAgent

  • ▸ Route to correct program (CalFresh, CalWORKs, etc.)
  • ▸ Priority classification
  • ▸ Confidence threshold gating
  • ▸ Human escalation triggers
STAGE 3

ActionAgent

  • ▸ Knowledge retrieval from CDSS policy index
  • ▸ FPL eligibility calculation
  • ▸ County office lookup
  • ▸ Response formatting with citations
